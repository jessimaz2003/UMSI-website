Study Spaces on Campus

At the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI), we understand the importance of having a conducive environment for studying and academic work. To support your academic success, we offer a variety of study spaces tailored to different needs and preferences. The University buildings provide a range of quiet, collaborative, and tech-equipped spaces designed to help you focus, collaborate, and access essential resources for your coursework. Whether you're looking for individual study rooms, group study areas, or spaces with advanced technology for research projects, you'll find the perfect spot.

Ross School of Business

The Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan is renowned for its stunning architectural design. With its expansive windows, the Winter Garden stands out for an abundance of natural light, creating a bright and inviting atmosphere. Inside, you'll find a Starbucks for your morning coffee, as well as a cafeteria offering a variety of dining options. The combination of beautiful architecture, natural lighting, and convenient amenities makes the Ross School of Business a perfect space for both work and relaxation.

School of Kinesiology

The School of Kinesiology at the University of Michigan boasts a unique and modern architectural design, highlighted by its striking skylight-style roof that floods the interior with natural light. The building's curved, contemporary layout creates an open and dynamic atmosphere, enhancing both functionality and aesthetic appeal. Inside, you'll find comfortable booths and couches perfect for studying or relaxing, making it a welcoming space for students to collaborate, unwind, or work on individual projects. The blend of innovative design and cozy seating makes the School of Kinesiology an ideal environment for both academic and social engagement.

Shapiro Undergraduate Library (UGLI)

The Undergraduate Library (UGLI) at the University of Michigan offers a vibrant and modern space perfect for students seeking both productivity and tranquility. The large windows throughout the library provide stunning views of the surrounding campus, creating a bright and inspiring environment. Recently renovated, the third floor now features state-of-the-art technologies, including noise-canceling booths designed for focused work or private calls. For those in need of a quiet, distraction-free environment, the UGLI also offers a silent room, providing an ideal space for deep concentration. With its combination of beautiful views, modern amenities, and quiet areas, the UGLI is a top choice for students seeking a versatile and peaceful study setting.

Law Library

The Law Library at the University of Michigan is a captivating, ornate space that feels like something out of a Harry Potter movie. The stunning stained glass windows cast a warm, colorful light across the room, while shelves lined with old books add a sense of history and tradition. The quiet, serene atmosphere makes it an ideal place for focused studying, allowing students to immerse themselves in their work. With its beautiful design and timeless charm, the Law Library offers a unique and inspiring setting that brings a touch of magic to the study experience.
